THE RULES
01
Phone across the room every night. No exceptions. This is how you actually wake up at 5am.
02
Games only after all blocks are done. Not during. Not "just 30 min first." After.
03
Transit time is productive time. Anki on the phone during all 4 hours of daily commute = ~1,000 Japanese cards/week.
04
Book writing = output only. No editing during the session. Just write. Fix it later.
05
Missed a block? Don't skip the next one. Just continue. Consistency beats perfection.
06
Weekends at mom's are protected. Do your morning blocks early and then fully enjoy the time.
